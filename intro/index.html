公司简介

联合动力有限公司

联合动力有限公司 (UnitePowers, LLC) 成立于2010年，是一家集产品研发集成、生产制造、销售于一体的高科技企业。本公司拥有先进的聚合物薄膜与新一代功能 TFC 固态复合膜材料核心设计技术与特色的组装工艺与生产制造能力，20年基于商业成功的新一代电活性储能电池与换能传感器核心产品研发实力，面向能源储能电源与医疗信息传感器中高端产业价值链市场，自主开发高附加值核心产品主要包括：模块化大容量 TFC 锂塑电芯 (Li-Polymer Cell) 及集成电池(iBattTM)储能系统 ，模块化高灵敏度 TFC 压塑电芯 (Piezo-Polymer Cell)集成模拟前端(iAFE)及医疗传感器信息系统。研制并开发出具有完全自主知识产权的核心产品系列与型号，并提供面向客户的解决方案与优质的技术质询及服务。

联合动力有限公司功能聚合物薄膜事业部集20年 TFC 及 TFN 复合薄膜材料的研发经验成果与科技结晶，依托先进材料研究院 (IAM)，有机结合强大的功能新材料产业园创新生产基地与高品质聚合物薄膜与复合膜制造工艺及生产能力，传承20年电活性储能换能产品商业与工业成功的产品设计新理念，以市场为导向，不断创新，推出新一代 TFC 薄膜材料、电极材料、数字与绿色工程新材料及结构与功能模块组件新产品系列，拥有活跃的地区合作伙伴及地区总代理。客户及业务范围覆盖能源环保与医疗健康产业市场，包括从便携式3C产品电源到电动单车动力电池与电信电力储能市场，智能信息医疗监护系统到智能家居与工业交通传感器物联网市场。

UnitePowers, LLC was founded in 2010. As a high-tech enterprise, UnitePowers, LLC possesses vertically integrated R&D, manufacturing and sales capabilities, including core design and cell packaging technology of advanced polymer film and functional thin-film composite (TFC and TFN) membrane, and own way to build up special technology product R&D pattern and manufacturing mode, specialized in designing and manufacturing TFC integrated scalable energy storage battery and conversion transducer products, which is based on 20-year electroactive membrane materials and energy storage and conversion products science and technology, beyond the business innovations and commercial and industrial successes of TFC membranes and processes technologies on plastic Li-ion battery and piezo-plastic electro-acoustic transducer. The core proprietary products include a series of modular large-capacity TFC Li-Polymer Cell and integrated battery (iBattTM) power sources and energy storage systems, and modular high-sensitivity Piezo-Polymer Cell and integrated Analog Front End (iAFE) for medical transducer information systems, with intellectual property, providing solutions to next-generation scalable energy storage and conversion and services for energy and medicare customers and markets.

Functional Polymer and Composite Membrane Manufacturing Department & Energy Storage and Conversion Product Department of UnitePowers, LLC continue focusing on core technology and capabilities for next-generation product development and manufacturing : developing and producing unique and value-added products from strategic TFC membranes, TFC electrode materials and cell packaging, to digital and green engineering materials, and a series of products of structural and functional modular components and assemblies, by transforming technology breakthrough of Institute of Advanced Materials (IAM), with integral production assembly lines of industrial park, strategic local partnership and sales representatives, for green energy, environment and medicare markets and clients.
